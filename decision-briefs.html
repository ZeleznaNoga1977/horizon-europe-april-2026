What is a Decision Brief?
A Decision Brief is a 12–18 page call intelligence document that decodes a Horizon Europe topic into actionable insight. It answers three questions: Should I apply? What do evaluators actually want? Who do I need in my consortium?

Every fact is verified against the EU Funding & Tenders Portal. Every analytical claim cites the specific source text it’s based on. Every strategic interpretation is clearly marked — so you always know what’s fact and what’s expert reading.

Each brief is produced using advanced AI analysis and reviewed by an experienced EU grant engineer to ensure accuracy, compliance, and strategic relevance.
